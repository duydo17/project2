Duge Bride Brige

Duge Bridge is the tallest bridge in the world recorded in 2021. It is 565 meters above sea level and spans the Beipan River. The bridge connects Guizhou with Yunnan and was completed in 2016. [see more...]

Millau Viaduct Briges

The Millau Viaduct is a cable-stayed viaduct that spans the valley of the River Tarn in Millau, Aveyron province, southern France. The bridge was designed by British architect Norman Foster and French bridge engineer Michel Virlogeux. This bridge was once considered the tallest in the world and is still the tallest bridge in the world today. [see more...]

Pingtang Briges

The tallest bridge in China. Located in Guizhou Province, the rope bridge spans a large gorge and is an important part of the Pingtang-Ladian highway. The main tower of the building is the tallest concrete column in the world. The local government has spent about US$215 million to build a project to encourage travel time reduction between Pingtang (Guizhou) and Luodian (Shanghai).
